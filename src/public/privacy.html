Privacy Policy

Welcome to Uplo, an end-to-end encrypted (e2ee) temporary file sharing service. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

  • Files are stored based on user preferences: removed after a set number of downloads, removed after a set amount of time, or stored indefinitely (though nothing is truly forever).

  • Details about how files are currently stored can be found on the homepage of our website. By prepending /info/ to a file request, you can view the parameters for that file.

  • We do not share your data with third parties unless required by law.

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

  • We take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or unauthorized destruction of the data.
